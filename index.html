About Javi

Who is Javi-air?

Javi started running when he was in middle school where he ran his first 100 meter hurdles, 200 meters and 100 meters. He continued his journey in running when he went into high school where he ran Cross Country and Track winning the regional 1st place in CC in 2013. Now, he continues with his running and races across the U.S. He completed his first 100 mile run in Leadville, Co. he continues running in ultra distances, trail and road.
